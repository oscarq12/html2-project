Men's Physique and Social Media

Men's physique, is a competition class in two of the most prestigious divisions. The divisions are the National Physique Committee, and Internation Federation of Bodybuilding. The sport/competition is not a maintstream subject of today's soceity. Both, I believed to be heavily impacted due to social media.

Social media, has the power to build up, expose or destroy anything with the simple actions of a few clicks. Unfortunately, doing competitions that involve a strong commitment to a diet program and a hyperthrophy routine are not very mainstream as playing football.

Digging Much Deeper

Social media, although can create a huge impact for an individual who competes and wants to share their experiences and trials through the competition preparation. Such as, inspiring others who follow the athlete on any platform that is public for viewing. These individuals will have created a fan base for others to come up to them for greetings and to take a photo with them.

There's More with Social Media!

One of the great tools with Social media, is being able to develop a business from it. Competitors tend to become online personal trainers, or contest prep coaches known to many others. The can earn quite an income that is sustainable for the athlete and can expand by their clients word of mouth and their journey.

Conclusion

Overall, Men's Physique can be quite the income when using social media and be wildly known so others around the world can know of this sport. Some look up to the ones competing and reach out to them for training. While others admire their hardwork and discipline to their craft.
